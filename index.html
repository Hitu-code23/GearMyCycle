About Us

THE GEARMYCYCLE STORY

Founded in 2025 in the heart of Jalandhar, GearMyCycle was born out of a simple realization: a bicycle is more than just a means of transport. It's an extension of the rider's personality, a tool for freedom, and a piece of engineering art.

OUR MISSION

We aim to bring premium cycling culture to India by providing high-quality, hand-assembled bikes that are built to last. Every cycle that leaves our workshop has been meticulously tuned and tested to ensure it meets our rigorous performance standards.

WHY WE DO IT

Whether you're a professional athlete looking for that extra edge, or a daily commuter seeking a reliable and stylish ride, we believe you deserve a machine that performs flawlessly. We're here to bridge the gap between mass-produced cycles and true cycling craftsmanship.

FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS

DO YOU SHIP OUTSIDE JALANDHAR?
Yes, we ship our bikes across India. Each bike is partially disassembled and packed securely to ensure it reaches you in perfect condition. Some basic assembly will be required upon arrival.
CAN I CUSTOMIZE MY BIKE?
Absolutely. Custom builds are our specialty. You can choose everything from the frame color to the specific groupset and wheel configuration. Contact us for a consultation.
WHAT KIND OF WARRANTY DO YOU OFFER?
All our frames come with a 5-year warranty against manufacturing defects. Components are covered by their respective manufacturer warranties, typically ranging from 1 to 2 years.
DO YOU OFFER SERVICING FOR OTHER BRANDS?
Yes, our workshop is equipped to handle professional servicing and repairs for all premium bicycle brands.
