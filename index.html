Got Questions?
WE HAVE
ANSWERS

Everything you need to know before, during, and after buying your GearMyCycle.

How do I choose the right bike for me?+
We offer a free 30-minute ride consultation — in person at our Jalandhar workshop or over WhatsApp. We'll ask about your riding style, terrain, fitness level, and budget to help you find the perfect match. Book a consultation here.
What sizes are your bikes available in?+
All our bikes come in S, M, L, and XL. For custom builds, we can fine-tune the geometry to your exact measurements — seat tube length, top tube length, reach, and stack. Getting sized properly is part of the service.
Can I test ride before buying?+
Absolutely. Visit us at our Jalandhar workshop and we'll set up a test ride on the model you're interested in. We'd never expect you to buy something you haven't felt under you first.
How often should I service my bike?+
For regular riders (4–5 days a week), we recommend a full service every 3 months. For casual riders, every 6 months. A basic checkup — brakes, gears, tyres — should be done monthly. We offer a free first service with every bike purchased.
Do you service bikes from other brands?+
Yes, we service all brands — Trek, Giant, Firefox, Hero, and more. Our mechanics are trained to handle most drivetrain and brake systems. Bring it in for a free assessment and we'll give you a quote.
How long does a repair usually take?+
Basic repairs (brake adjustment, gear tuning, puncture fix) are typically same-day. More involved work like wheel rebuilding or full drivetrain overhauls take 2–3 days. We'll always give you a timeline upfront.
How does the custom build process work?+
It starts with a consultation call or visit. We discuss your riding goals, preferred style, budget, and aesthetics. Then we source components, build a spec sheet for your approval, and begin fabrication. The full process typically takes 3–4 weeks from deposit to delivery.
Can I choose my own paint colour and finish?+
Yes — custom colour is included in all full custom builds. You can choose from matte, satin, gloss, or raw finishes. We work with a local painter who specialises in bicycle finishing. You can also bring your own reference image or colour swatch.
What is the minimum budget for a custom build?+
Custom builds start from ₹ 30,000. This covers a basic chromoly frame with entry-level components. Higher-spec builds with premium drivetrains, suspension forks, or carbon parts go from ₹ 55,000 upward. We work within your budget and are always transparent about what each price point gets you.
Do you deliver across India?+
Yes! We ship fully assembled and boxed bikes across India via courier. Delivery typically takes 5–7 business days. Jalandhar and nearby Punjab cities can arrange free local delivery. Shipping costs vary by location — contact us for a quote.
Is there a warranty on bikes?+
All GearMyCycle bikes come with a 1-year warranty on the frame and 6 months on components against manufacturing defects. Custom builds come with our lifetime relationship — if something's wrong, bring it in and we'll sort it.
Still have questions?

Can't find what you're looking for? We're just a message away. Reach out on WhatsApp or drop us an email.

WHY GEARMYCYCLE
EXISTS

GearMyCycle was born from frustration — with bicycles that looked the same, felt the same, and inspired nothing. We believed that a bike should feel like an extension of who you are: sharp, intentional, alive.

Founded in the streets of Jalandhar, GearMyCycle started as a workshop with one philosophy: if you're going to put something in motion, make it extraordinary.

We're not the biggest. We don't want to be. We want to be the brand that people remember the first time they rode something that felt truly theirs.
