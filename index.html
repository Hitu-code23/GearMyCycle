Get your fit right before the first ride. A well-fitted bike is safer, faster, and more enjoyable.

01
Standover clearance first. When standing flat-footed over the top tube, you should have 2–5 cm of clearance for MTB, 1–3 cm for road bikes. This is the most critical safety check.
02
Saddle height. With your heel on the pedal at its lowest point, your leg should be almost straight. This puts your knee at roughly 30° of bend when pedalling with the ball of your foot.
03
Reach and handlebar drop. You should be able to ride with a slight bend in the elbows — never locked straight. For comfort riding, a more upright position is ideal. For performance, a longer, lower reach.
04
Knee over pedal. At the 3 o'clock position, a plumb line from your knee should fall directly over the pedal axle. This maximises power transfer and reduces knee strain.
05
Between two sizes? Go smaller for aggressive / technical riding. Go larger for long-distance or comfort riding. Or visit our Jalandhar workshop — we offer in-person fitting consultations for free.

About Us

THE GEARMYCYCLE STORY

Founded in 2025 in the heart of Jalandhar, GearMyCycle was born out of a simple realization: a bicycle is more than just a mode of transport—it's an extension of the rider's personality and a tool for freedom.

We started as a small workshop dedicated to restoring vintage frames and have since grown into Jalandhar's premier destination for high-performance cycling. Our philosophy remains the same: meticulous attention to detail, uncompromising quality, and a passion for the ride.

OUR MISSION

To provide every cyclist with a machine that is perfectly tuned to their needs, whether they are navigating city streets, conquering mountain trails, or embarking on long-distance adventures.

Support

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do you offer custom builds?
Yes! We specialize in custom bike building. You can choose your frame, components, and even custom paint jobs. Contact us for a consultation.
What is the warranty on your bikes?
Most of our frames come with a lifetime warranty against manufacturing defects. Components carry their respective manufacturer warranties, typically 1-2 years.
Do you ship across India?
Currently, we focus on serving the Jalandhar and Punjab region to ensure the best possible assembly and support. However, we can arrange shipping for specific models—please enquire.
How often should I service my bike?
For regular riders, we recommend a basic tune-up every 3-6 months and a full overhaul once a year to keep everything running smoothly and safely.
How do I find the right frame size?
Use our interactive size guide to get a recommendation based on your height and inseam, or visit our workshop for a personal fitting session.
